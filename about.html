Our Mission: Your Career Launchpad

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world industry demands by providing students with affordable, hands-on internship projects guided by expert mentors.

Our Vision

A world where every technology student graduates with the practical experience, portfolio, and confidence needed to excel in their career from day one.

Our Story

Algonive was born from a simple but powerful frustration. Like many tech students, our journey was filled with textbooks and lectures, but when it came time to apply for our first real-world jobs, we hit a wall. We saw a huge gap between our classroom knowledge and what companies were actually looking for: practical skills and a portfolio of completed projects.

That frustration sparked an idea: What if we could build the platform we wish we had? A place where students could stop just learning and start doing.
