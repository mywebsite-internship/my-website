Your Questions, Answered

Here are some of the most common questions we get.

Is this internship remote or in-office? What are the timings?

All our internships are 100% remote, allowing you to work from anywhere. The timings are completely flexible. Our programs are self-paced, which means you can complete your tasks on your own schedule and easily fit the internship around your studies or other commitments.

Are these internships paid?

"While the primary goal of our internships is to help you learn by building real-world projects, we reward our top performers. The best-performing interns are eligible for a stipend of up to ₹12,000 based on their overall performance. Regardless of the stipend, our focus is on delivering value to all interns through expert mentorship, portfolio-ready projects, and official certification."

Are there any prerequisites?

Each internship has different requirements, which are listed on the application form. Generally, we recommend having a basic understanding of the domain (e.g., basic Python for Data Science, basic HTML/CSS for Web Development) to get the most out of the program.

How long is the internship?

Choose the duration that works for you. We offer 1-month, 2-month, and 3-month internship tracks. Each program is self-paced and fully flexible, allowing you to complete tasks on your own schedule and fit them around your studies.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes! Upon successful completion and submission of all assigned tasks and your final project, you will receive an official, verifiable Certificate of Internship Completion from Algonive. This is perfect for sharing on your LinkedIn profile and resume.
